Reminders on Submitting Stories

Choose Registered Case:
If the story has already been recorded by the authorities, and have already investigated the case.

Choose Extra-terrestrial:
If the story is about the unknown beyond earthly facts, characters, or unidentified flying objects.

Choose Uninvestigated:
If the story is a new discovery where there have been no authorities involved and have never been recorded by the police. This can be a story only you know..
