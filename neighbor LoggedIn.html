I Saw My Neighbor's Killers

I had just returned home from work. My husband was relaxing on our enclosed front porch. As I returned from the bathroom, my husband told me two guys had just walked in between our house and the neighbor's. He told me that last time that happened, the neighbor's house got broken into. I told him to keep an eye on things… I went to the kitchen to start dinner, and my husband shouted “do you hear that screaming?!?” I hadn't, so I opened our back door onto our screened in porch, which gave me a good view of the neighbor's back yard. I could see their back door was opened, and I heard lots of shouting, as well as my female neighbor screaming. I yelled at my husband to call the cops, and as he was doing so I heard 3 gunshots. Then I saw the two guys who had walked in between our houses run out, one holding a gun, which he then put in his waistband and under his shirt. The two guys ran into the alley while I was feeding information to the cops through my husband. My neighbor was still screaming. My male neighbor died en route to the hospital, after having been shot in front of his girlfriend and his 6 year old son. The two guys were captured a few weeks later, in another state. I had to testify at the murder trial. I never heard how the case was decided.
